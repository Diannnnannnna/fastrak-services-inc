Celebrating a legacy of over 26 years marked by excellence and innovation

FSI - Fastrak Services, Inc. stands as a distinguished DICT Licensed and PEMEDES Accredited Private Courier Company. As a leading delivery partner to major banks and credit card companies, FSI has been a consistent recipient of the Platinum and Titanium awards from Credit Card Association of the Philippines (CCAP) , showcasing unwavering commitment to industry standards.

Entering its 26th year in industry, FSI reflects on a remarkable journey of delivering approximately 400 million items (documents, mails & small parcels) across the country, spanning from Tuguegarao City in Cagayan Valley down to Davao City in Mindanao and reaching key cities and provinces in Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Through adherence to Bangko Central’s policies and procedures, FSI has solidified its position as a trusted partner in the essential mail and document delivery

Moreover, FSI has recently expanded its reach through a strategic partnership with SKYWARD, an e-Commerce Technology Solutions Company. This collaboration has propelled FSI to serve as a major courier and delivery partner for both Private and Key Government Institutions.
